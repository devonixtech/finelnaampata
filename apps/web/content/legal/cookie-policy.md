# 6\. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

## 6\.1 CMP Preference Centre \(Recommended Method\)

The most effective and comprehensive method to manage all Tracking Technology preferences is via our CMP Preference Centre at www\.naampata\.com/cookie\-settings\. You can: enable or disable specific non\-essential categories; withdraw any previously given consent; and update preferences at any time with immediate effect\.

## 6\.2 Browser\-Level Controls

- Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and Other Site Data
- Mozilla Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- Apple Safari: Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
- Microsoft Edge: Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data
- Opera: Settings → Privacy & security → Cookies

Important: Blocking Strictly Necessary cookies will prevent you from logging in and will cause the web application to malfunction\. Browser\-level cookie deletion does not clear non\-cookie local storage or session storage\. Clearing cookies resets your consent record — the consent banner will be re\-displayed on your next visit\.

## 6\.3 Google Analytics Opt\-Out \(Cross\-Site\)

To prevent Google Analytics from collecting your data across all websites: install the Google Analytics Opt\-out Browser Add\-on at https://tools\.google\.com/dlpage/gaoptout\. This prevents the GA JavaScript \(analytics\.js, gtag\.js\) from sending data to Google Analytics for your browser sessions\.

## 6\.4 Mobile Device Settings

- iOS: Settings → Safari → Privacy & Security → Block All Cookies \(note: may break some website functionality\)\. iOS App Tracking Transparency: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"\.
- Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation\. Google account: myaccount\.google\.com/data\-and\-privacy → Ad settings\.

# 7\. Affiliate Attribution Tracking

When a Business accesses the Platform via an Affiliate participant's referral link, NAAMPATA places a first\-party attribution cookie \(naampata\_aff\) with a 10\-day attribution window from the click event\. This cookie is used EXCLUSIVELY to attribute the subsequent Business signup to the correct Affiliate participant for commission calculation under the Affiliate & Commission Policy \(Document 8\)\. Server\-side referral code entry by the Business during signup takes ABSOLUTE precedence over cookie attribution — if both a valid attribution cookie and a manually entered referral code are present, the manually entered code governs without exception\.

# 8\. Web Device Fingerprinting \(Fraud Prevention Only\)

For fraud prevention, enforcement of permanent account bans against re\-registration, and detection of coordinated abusive behaviour patterns, our web application may collect browser and device signals to generate a probabilistic device identifier\. Signals may include: browser type and version; operating system and version; screen resolution and colour depth; time zone and language settings; font availability \(Canvas API\); and WebGL renderer information\. This processing is conducted EXCLUSIVELY for security and fraud prevention — it is NOT used for advertising targeting, commercial user profiling, or third\-party data sharing \(except with fraud\-prevention sub\-processors under strict data processing agreements\)\. Lawful basis: Legitimate Interests \(GDPR Art\. 6\(1\)\(f\)\) — LIA documented\.
