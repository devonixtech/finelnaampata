## 4\.1 Dishonest, Fraudulent & Manipulative Content

- False, fabricated, AI\-generated, incentivised, purchased, exchanged, bribed, coordinated, or plagiarised reviews, ratings, check\-ins, or Q&A entries\.
- Reviews for Businesses not genuinely and personally patronised; reviews for Businesses you own, manage, co\-own, financially invest in, or have any personal or financial association with\.
- Competitive sabotage — submitting, commissioning, or coordinating negative reviews to damage a competitor's reputation or search ranking\.
- Fictitious Business listings, duplicate mirror listings, or listings created for the purpose of manipulating search results or discovery\.
- Keyword stuffing, category misrepresentation, or any other technique to artificially manipulate search ranking\.
- Fabricated professional credentials, unverified certifications, false awards, misleading qualifications, or unsubstantiated superiority claims\.
- AI\-generated, digitally manipulated, or stock photographs misrepresented or implied as authentic Business imagery\.

## 4\.2 Illegal, Harmful & Dangerous Content

- Content unlawful under any applicable law, including content violating PECA 2016, the Pakistan Penal Code, the Defamation Ordinance 2002, the Competition Act 2010, or applicable consumer protection law\.
- Content promoting, facilitating, funding, glorifying, or inciting illegal activities, terrorism, extremism, violence, or sedition of any kind\.
- Child Sexual Abuse Material \(CSAM\) or any Content that sexualises, exploits, grooms, endangers, or harms minors in any way whatsoever\. See Section 9 — mandatory law enforcement reporting applies without exception\.
- Content facilitating, promoting, or advertising human trafficking, bonded labour, forced labour, organ trafficking, or any form of human exploitation\.
- Content that promotes, glorifies, facilitates, or provides instructions for self\-harm or suicide\.
- Content related to goods or services in categories absolutely prohibited under the Business Terms of Service \(Document 3\), Section 3\.

## 4\.3 Harassing, Abusive & Discriminatory Content

- Harassment, bullying, stalking, threatening, intimidating, menacing, or abusing any individual, group, User, Business, or NAAMPATA personnel, whether through public Content or private chat\.
- Hate speech, discriminatory content, or content promoting hostility or violence based on religion, ethnicity, nationality, gender, sexual orientation, disability, age, caste, or any other protected characteristic\.
- Doxxing — publishing, referencing, or threatening to publish private or personal information about any individual without their explicit consent\.
- Defamatory content — false statements of fact about any identifiable person or Business that damage their reputation\.

## 4\.4 Spam & Unsolicited Content

- Spam of any kind, including bulk unsolicited messages, phishing communications, fraudulent impersonation communications, or misleading promotional messages\.

# 5\. Review Integrity Standards

__Standard__

__Rule & Enforcement Detail__

Daily Maximum

3 reviews per day per registered User account, across the ENTIRE Platform \(not per Business\)

Genuine First\-Hand Experience

Reviews must reflect genuine, first\-hand, personal commercial experience with the specific Business reviewed\. Generic, speculative, or second\-hand reviews are prohibited\.

No Self\-Review

Businesses may not review themselves or arrange, request, or incentivise any associated person to submit reviews\.

Zero Tolerance on Incentivisation

Absolutely zero tolerance for reviews submitted or solicited in exchange for payment, free goods, discounts, services, gift cards, or any other benefit — no exceptions\.

No Competitor Manipulation

Submitting or commissioning negative reviews to damage a competitor constitutes fraud and may violate the Competition Act 2010\.

Algorithmic Fraud Detection

Automated analysis of: review velocity; linguistic duplication; IP and device clustering; account age; Trust Score signals; behavioural anomalies\. ALL flagged reviews require mandatory human moderator review before any action\.

Banned Account Reviews

Where a User is permanently banned for confirmed review fraud, NAAMPATA may at its discretion remove, suppress, or flag reviews submitted by that account to protect platform review integrity, regardless of prior anonymisation\.

Photo Reviews

Up to 5 review photographs per review\. Auto\-compressed to WebP on upload\. Moderated independently from review text\. Review photos may be rejected without the review itself being rejected\.
