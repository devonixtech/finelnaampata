# 7\. Holding Period, Auto\-Reversal & Payouts

## 7\.1 30\-Calendar\-Day Holding Period

All earned commissions enter a mandatory 30\-calendar\-day holding period beginning on the date of the qualifying payment\. During this period, NAAMPATA verifies: \(i\) the payment has not been reversed, refunded, charged back, or disputed; \(ii\) no fraud signals or policy violations have been detected in connection with the referral; and \(iii\) the Affiliate's KYC is complete, current, and approved by Finance Admin\.

## 7\.2 Auto\-Reversal Events

Commission is automatically reversed \(permanently set to zero and rendered ineligible for payout\) if, during the holding period: \(a\) the referred Business's qualifying payment is refunded, charged back, reversed, or cancelled by the Business for any reason; or \(b\) NAAMPATA determines — at any time, including after payout — that the referral was obtained through fraud, self\-referral, a prohibited promotional method, code sharing, cookie stuffing, or any other violation of this Policy\. Where fraud is confirmed after a payout has already been processed, NAAMPATA will recover the commission amount from the Affiliate through direct offset against future commissions, against any credit balance, or through a direct recovery demand under applicable law\.
