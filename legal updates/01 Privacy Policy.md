# 13\. Device Fingerprinting for Fraud Prevention

For fraud prevention, enforcement of permanent account bans against re\-registration, and detection of coordinated abuse patterns, our web application may collect browser and device signals to generate a probabilistic device identifier \("device fingerprint"\)\. Signals may include: browser type and version; operating system and version; screen resolution and colour depth; time zone; language preferences; and network connectivity type\. Device fingerprinting is used exclusively for: \(i\) detecting fake reviews, fake accounts, and affiliate fraud; \(ii\) enforcing permanent bans against re\-registration by banned users; and \(iii\) identifying coordinated abusive behaviour\. It is NOT used for advertising targeting, commercial profiling, or sharing with third parties other than fraud\-prevention sub\-processors under strict data processing obligations\. Lawful basis: Legitimate Interests \(GDPR Art\. 6\(1\)\(f\)\) — LIA documented\.
